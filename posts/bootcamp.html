AI/ML Bootcamp Journey

In 2025, I had the opportunity to teach an 8-week AI/ML bootcamp for teens. This page documents my journey, the curriculum, and the resources we used.

my teaching philosophy

When designing this bootcamp, I focused on three key principles:

  • Hands-on learning through practical projects
  • Building intuition before diving into complex theory
  • Making AI/ML accessible and fun for young learners

weekly curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to AI & Python Basics
Week 1: What is AI?
Week 2: Data & Visualization
Week 3: Machine Learning Basics
Week 4: Neural Networks
Week 5: Deep Learning
Week 6: Natural Language Processing
Week 7: Reinforcement Learning
Week 8: Final Projects

key learnings

Teaching this bootcamp taught me several valuable lessons:

  • The importance of breaking down complex concepts into digestible pieces
  • The value of hands-on projects in reinforcing theoretical concepts
  • How to make technical content engaging for young learners

resources

Here are some of the key resources we used:

  • Google Colab
  • Google Teachable Machine
  • Kaggle Learn
  • Fast.ai
